Holi: The "Festival of Colors," it marks the arrival of spring, the victory of good over evil, and the love between Radha and Krishna.

Navratri: A nine-night festival that celebrates the divine feminine power and victory of goddess Durga.

Durga Puja: A major festival in West Bengal and other parts of India that commemorates the victory of goddess Durga over the demon Mahishasura.

Ganesh Chaturthi: Celebrates the birth of Lord Ganesha and is particularly prominent in Maharashtra.

Onam: A harvest festival celebrated in Kerala.

Pongal: Another harvest festival celebrated in Tamil Nadu, typically over four days in mid-January.

Maha Shivaratri: Celebrates the marriage of Lord Shiva and Parvati.

Eid-ul-Fitr: Marks the end of the holy month of Ramadan.

Christmas: Celebrated by Christians across the country.
